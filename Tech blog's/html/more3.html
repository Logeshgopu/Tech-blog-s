JIO AIR FIBER

Jio unveils new plan with Swiggy One Lite subscription bundle

Jio's new wireless internet service, Jio AirFiber, brings high-speed connectivity to areas where wired connections are a challenge. The service offers impressive speeds, easy installation, and extensive coverage compared to Jio Fiber.Jio AirFiber now available in 8 cities offering upto 1Gbps speed, free Disney Plus Hotstar subscription and more.

Jio has launched its new wireless internet service, JioAirFiber, in eight cities across the country. The service offers six plans with high-speed internet, OTT benefits and other features.

From UPI to the digitization of government facilities, fast-speed internet has become a necessity for people to continue working. However, the mobile network is often unreliable, and cable fiber networks are not available everywhere. Therefore, to help people achieve faster speeds with a wireless connection, Jio finally started offering Jio AirFiber in September 2023.

Jio AirFiber is a wireless internet service from Jio that uses 5G technology to provide high-speed internet connectivity. It is designed to meet the needs of home entertainment, smart home services, and high-speed broadband. It offers speeds comparable to those of traditional fiber-optic connections, and users can also access speeds of up to 1 Gbps.

Jio states that Jio AirFiber is similar to wireless 4G dongles but with additional benefits, such as broadband-like speeds via 5G. Users can choose from 30Mbps, 100Mbps, 150Mbps, 300Mbps, 500Mbps, and 1Gbps plans.

Jio Airfiber available in these cities
Jio is rolling out the AirFiber service nationwide. However, as of the initial launch, the service is available in only eight cities: Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai, and Pune.

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to book a JioAirfiber connection:
step1: Check availability. Go to the Jio website, My Jio app, or contact Jio customer support to check if Jio AirFiber is available in your area.

step2: Initiate booking. You can book a new connection by giving a missed call to 60008-60008, visiting the Jio website or My Jio app, or visiting your nearest Jio Store.

step3: Register for Jio AirFiber. Provide the required details to complete the registration process.

step4: Await confirmation. Jio will contact you when the services become available in your building or location.

step5: Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive your Jio AirFiber connection, which includes a WiFi router, 4K Smart Set-Top Box, and a voice-activated remote. An outdoor unit will also be installed on your terrace/rooftop or outside your home.
